ABOUT US

Remmamy Catering Services believes in exceptionally satisfying clients and making gustatory delights a reality! Be it food you are craving for or an innovative dish or event idea, we work with you to make it happen.

Good food begins with quality ingredients, and that's where Remmamy Catering starts every meal. With a focus on organic and farm-fresh produce and meats, we consider only the best quality ingredients before we decide to incorporate them into our catering services. We avoid the pre-packaged, frozen, canned ingredients and collect only the freshest and finest. Fresh and wholesome is our standard in fact, we purchase most of our vegetables from the farm next door to us.

We offers great satisfaction and awesome experience not only in food but in all of their services that guarantee the customers needs. Through the great effort of their kitchen personnel and their catering crews they are successfully giving the services and a high quality value of foods that suites to all occasions and theme.

Wanting to give their clients service and a catering experience like no other, Remmamy Catering has always strived to provide something new to their patrons. This is evident in the constantly expanding menus and packages that they offer. “We attend seminars and trainings in order to see the newest trends that we can use here in the country.” Remmamy Catering believes that we have to continuously learn new approaches in providing for the needs.
